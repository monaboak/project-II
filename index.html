Wild Fires

Los Angeles is suffers from natural forest fires. These fires go two ways- they run their course and burn out within a matter of hours, or they take on a life of their own and engulf dozens of cities in the Southern Californian region.

Car Culture

Los Angeles differs from other populous cities -like New York, for example- in that it has a huge car culture. This crazed culture comes from the fact that L.A. is a metropolitan area, and the county is about 4,700 miles wide. The wide mapped layout of the county, dense population, and high traffic is what contributes to the Los Angeles car culture being dangerous. Cars sitting in traffic causes a high level of car emission, which then produces a great deal of smog and eventually creates a hole in the ozone layer.
